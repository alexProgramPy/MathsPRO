How to Play MathsPRO Quiz!
Welcome to MathsPRO Quiz! Follow these easy steps to start playing and have fun while improving your math skills:

Step-by-Step Guide:
Launch the app:

Open the game application on your device.
Select a Quiz:

You can choose from a variety of quizzes. Each quiz has different types of math questions.
Options may include Algebra, BODMAS, times tables, division, or a mix of all.
Choose Difficulty Level:

The game offers multiple difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard.
Select the difficulty level you want to play. Remember, the higher the difficulty, the more challenging the questions will be.
Understand the Challenge:

Each quiz consists of 30 questions.
The time limit to complete these questions decreases as you choose higher difficulty levels.
Easy: You have more time to think and answer.
Medium: Slightly less time to answer each question.
Hard: Even less time, making it more challenging.

Start the Quiz:

Once you’ve selected your quiz and difficulty level, click the “Start Quiz” button.
A timer will start, and you will be presented with the first question.
Answer the Questions:

Read each question carefully and type your answer in the provided field.
Submit your answer to move on to the next question.
Try to answer as quickly and accurately as possible to beat the timer.
Complete the Quiz:

After answering all 30 questions, the game will show your score.
If the time runs out before you complete all the questions, the quiz will end automatically, and your score will be based on the number of correct answers you provided.
Review Your Score:

At the end of the quiz, you will see your score and the correct answers for each question.
Use this information to learn from any mistakes and improve your math skills.
Play Again:

Feel free to try another quiz or increase the difficulty level to challenge yourself further.s
The more you play, the better you’ll get!
Tips for Success:
Start with an easier difficulty to get comfortable with the game.
Practice regularly to improve your speed and accuracy.
Challenge yourself by trying higher difficulty levels as you get better.
Enjoy playing this fun maths game and have fun learning!
