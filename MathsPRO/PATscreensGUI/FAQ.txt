Frequently asked Questions (FAQ):

1. Is the app free to use?
Yes the app is completely free, there are no subscriptions or payments in the app. There is no pay to win advantage what so ever.

2. Does the app provide explanations for the solutions?
There is no explanation for the solution due to the questions being relatively simple to complete.

3. Is the app updated regularly?
The app will be updated regularly and information will be given as to what improvements will be add and users will be notified of any new content.

4. Can I use the app offline?
The app can be used offline and requires no internet connection. Internet connection is only required if users wish to update the app.

5. What educational levels does the app support?
The app is aimed towards teenagers up, so from at least grade 7 and above is required to perform well in the tests and complete the quizzes. The app requires a fair amoount of knowledge in high school algebra and mathematics.
